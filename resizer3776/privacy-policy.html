How Resizer 3776 handles user information.

Effective date: January 21, 2026 Last updated: January 21, 2026

1. Overview

Resizer 3776 is a smartphone application provided by Lab 3776. This policy explains how user information is handled within the app.

2. Information Collected and Purpose of Use

2.1 Storage and image access

The app accesses image files or archive files selected by the user in order to resize, compress, split, or preview content. These files are processed temporarily on the device and are not transmitted to or stored on Lab 3776 servers.

2.2 Advertising (AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may automatically collect information such as advertising identifiers, cookies, and approximate location data according to Google policies.

See the Google Ads Policy for more details.

3. Personal Information

Except for ad delivery by AdMob, the app does not collect or store personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email address.

4. Disclaimer

Lab 3776 is not responsible for damages caused by unexpected app malfunctions. This includes image quality degradation or loss of data resulting from processing. Important files should be backed up before use.
